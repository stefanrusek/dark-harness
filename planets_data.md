## Notes

- **Terrestrial planets** are small, rocky planets located closer to the Sun
- **Gas Giants** (Jupiter, Saturn) are massive planets composed primarily of gases
- **Ice Giants** (Uranus, Neptune) are smaller than gas giants and contain more "ices" (water, ammonia, methane)
- Moon counts are as of the latest astronomical surveys (2024)
